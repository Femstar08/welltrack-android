WellTrack Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Last Updated: January 1, 2025

WellTrack ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use, including our integration with health platforms like Garmin Connect.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Health and Fitness Data

We collect health and fitness information that you provide directly or through connected health platforms:

  • Manual health entries (weight, blood pressure, medications, etc.)
  • Meal and nutrition information
  • Exercise and activity logs
  • Health goals and preferences
  • Biomarker and lab result data

Garmin Connect Integration

When you choose to connect your Garmin account, we collect health and fitness data from your Garmin devices through the Garmin Connect platform, including:

  • Heart Rate Variability (HRV) measurements
  • Training Recovery Scores and fitness metrics
  • Stress level measurements and Body Battery data
  • VO2 Max and biological/fitness age calculations
  • Sleep quality metrics and sleep stages
  • Activity and exercise data from workouts
Important: Garmin data collection occurs only after you explicitly authorize the connection through Garmin Connect's secure OAuth process. You maintain full control over this connection and can revoke access at any time.

1.2 Account Information

  • Email address and account credentials
  • Profile information (name, age, gender, height)
  • App preferences and settings

1.3 Usage Information

  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Device information and operating system details
  • Crash reports and performance data (anonymized)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Purposes

  • Provide personalized health insights and recommendations
  • Track progress toward your health and fitness goals
  • Generate comprehensive health reports and analytics
  • Sync and integrate data across multiple health platforms
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

Garmin Data Usage

Your Garmin data is used specifically to:

  • Provide personalized health insights based on HRV, recovery, and stress data
  • Track fitness progress and biological age trends
  • Combine with other health sources for comprehensive analysis
  • Generate reports that include proper Garmin attribution as required by Garmin brand guidelines

Data Attribution: All displays of Garmin-sourced data include proper attribution (e.g., "Garmin [device model]") as required by Garmin API Brand Guidelines. This ensures transparency about data sources and maintains compliance with Garmin partnership requirements.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 General Policy

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal health information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent for specific purposes
  • In aggregated, anonymized form for research and improvement
  • As required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, safety, or the safety of others

Garmin Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your Garmin data with third parties for marketing purposes. Garmin data may be shared only:

  • With your explicit consent (e.g., sharing reports with healthcare providers)
  • In exported reports that maintain required Garmin attribution
  • In aggregated, anonymized form for health research (with proper attribution)
  • As required by law or to protect rights and safety

4. Data Security and Encryption

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • AES-256 encryption for data storage
  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Biometric authentication options for app access

5. Data Retention and Deletion

5.1 General Retention Policy

We retain your data for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide services. You may delete your data at any time through the app settings.

Garmin Data Retention

Your Garmin data is retained according to the following policies:

  • Data is retained while your account is active and Garmin connection is authorized
  • You can delete Garmin data at any time through Settings > Privacy & Data > Delete Garmin Data
  • When you revoke Garmin access, new data collection stops but existing data is preserved unless manually deleted
  • Complete account deletion removes all Garmin data within 30 days
  • Historical data exports and reports retain Garmin attribution as required by guidelines

6. Your Rights and Controls

6.1 Data Access and Control

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access and review all collected data
  • Correct or update inaccurate information
  • Delete specific data points or entire datasets
  • Export your data in standard formats
  • Control data sharing and privacy settings

Garmin Connection Management

You maintain full control over your Garmin connection:

To Disconnect Garmin in WellTrack:

  1. Go to Settings > Connected Apps
  2. Select Garmin and choose "Disconnect"
  3. Confirm disconnection (stops new data sync)

To Revoke Access in Garmin Connect:

  1. Open Garmin Connect app or visit connect.garmin.com
  2. Go to Account Settings > App Management
  3. Find WellTrack and select "Remove Access"

To Delete Garmin Data:

  1. Go to Settings > Privacy & Data
  2. Select "Delete Garmin Data"
  3. Confirm deletion (this cannot be undone)

9. International Data Transfers
