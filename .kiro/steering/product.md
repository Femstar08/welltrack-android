# WellTrack Product Overview

WellTrack is a comprehensive health and wellness Android application that helps users track their nutrition, fitness, and overall well-being through integrated health data management.

## Core Features

- **Meal Tracking & Planning**: Log meals, create meal plans, and track nutritional intake
- **Recipe Management**: Store recipes with OCR scanning, URL import, and manual entry
- **Health Integration**: Connect with Garmin, Samsung Health, and Android Health Connect
- **Supplement Tracking**: Monitor supplement intake and effectiveness
- **Shopping & Pantry**: Manage shopping lists and pantry inventory with expiry tracking
- **Goal Setting**: Set and track health and fitness goals
- **Data Export**: Export personal health data for portability
- **Social Features**: Share recipes and achievements with other users

## Target Users

Health-conscious individuals who want a unified platform to track their nutrition, fitness, and wellness data across multiple devices and platforms.

## Key Value Propositions

1. **Unified Health Data**: Aggregates data from multiple health platforms
2. **Comprehensive Tracking**: Covers nutrition, fitness, supplements, and lifestyle
3. **Privacy-First**: User data ownership with secure local and cloud storage
4. **Cross-Platform Integration**: Works with major health ecosystems
5. **Accessibility**: Full accessibility compliance for inclusive usage

## Business Model

The app appears to be designed as a premium health tracking solution with potential for subscription-based features and health data insights.
